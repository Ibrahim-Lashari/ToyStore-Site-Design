About Us

Woodland Wonders is a family-owned business founded by Sarah and Michael Evergreen. With a background in education and carpentry, they are dedicated to crafting eco-friendly wooden toys that inspire creativity in children.

Our Mission

At Woodland Wonders, we strive to ignite children's imagination through beautifully crafted wooden toys. Our commitment to sustainability, quality, and fostering creativity is at the heart of everything we do. Join us in creating lasting childhood memories and a brighter, eco-conscious future.
